whats new

  • New School Cafeteria

    After some meetings and some time the school remodaled the cafeteria to give it a modern look. They did this after some suggestions from teacher and students. The students liked the new cafeteria after the construction was done.

  • Implemented Online Attendence Management System

    After going virtual, school started doing everything virtual including attendence. This system allowed teacher to not have to use paper attendence. The school did this after some complaints about not having enough sheets and them being difficult to read. After the change it was easier for teachers to use.
